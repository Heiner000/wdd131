Site Name:

FitForge

Site Purpose:

FitForge is a Gym Buddy Workout Planner website designed to help users create personalized workout plans featuring progressive overload. It provides a user-friendly platform for fitness enthusiasts to generate custom routines based on available equipment and access a comprehensive exercise library.

Scenarios:

  • "I don't have any equipment, how can I get a good workout in?"
  • "With the equipment available to me, what exercises should I do?"
  • "I want to do a full body workout today with the selected equipment, what could I do?"
